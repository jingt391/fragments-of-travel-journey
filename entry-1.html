Flower

Visiting Victoria Island is like stepping into a storybook, its charm unfolding with every cobbled street and scenic harbor. My mom and I enjoyed afternoon tea at a quaint tea house nestled in a lush garden. The clinking of cups intertwined with the soothing aroma of freshly brewed tea and warm scones. Just as I savored the moment, the waiter approached me with a smile and handed me a fresh flower, its petals blooming with vibrant colors in the soft ambiance. Touched by this simple yet profound gesture, I carefully tucked the flower away and carried it with me, reminding myself of the gentle beauty and kindness of this island that made this experience unforgettable.
