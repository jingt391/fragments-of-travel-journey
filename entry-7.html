Traveling to Interlaken and then on to the Jungfrau in Switzerland is an unforgettable journey through some of the most stunning scenery I have ever seen. Nestled between the tranquil lakes of Thun and Brienz, Interlaken is the perfect gateway to the majestic Swiss Alps. The surrounding landscape is so pure and pristine that it seems untouched by age. To commemorate this experience, I kept my passport stamp from the Jungfrau, a tangible reminder that I stood on top of Europe. The stamp is small but significant, it represents a moment of awe and achievement, preserving the memory of that magical journey forever.
